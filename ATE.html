I believe there is nothing more precious on this Earth than the spirit of a child, no matter how old. In an attempt to help nurture and cultivate strong leaders of tomorrow, I spend my today lending a smile and a helping hand to our future.

I have had the pleasure of working with students in Tennessee and in Texas. I have been Ms. Angela the friend, teacher, mentor, and most of all cheerleader for students of all backgrounds and ethnicities. It is my goal to be sure that children have solid footing and consistency in their lives.

The only way the sun will come up bright tomorrow is if we safely guide it to safety today.
