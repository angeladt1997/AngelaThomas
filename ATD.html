I am a classically trained choreographer, specializing in the art forms of ballet, modern, and improvisation. I like to compose pieces to stand-out musical scores where dancers push their bodies to the limits. In each piece I produce, each dancer gets creative voice and choice on their involvement of the choreographic process and the performance of the work. Most of my movement stems from my fascination with architecture and the mannerisms of people around me. My vision has been known to test the limits of my own body, the physicality of movers and critical thinking of audiences across multiple states.
