Angela The Artist

If a form of art exists, I want to learn it

Black History Painting Completed in February 2020

Since the age of 8, I have been trying to get as involved in the arts as possible. My journey started by chance. A family member took me to our local theater where I got to see my first staged work A Raisin In The Sun. I sat glued to my chair as I watched this new world unfold in front of me. From there, I became active in the community theater. I then developed a love for dance, visual arts, and vocal music. Though I am only active in a few art mediums right now, I am always looking for new avenues of expression.
