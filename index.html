About the Founder

Angela Thomas is a Mississippi native looking to make her mark on the world from Dallas, Texas.

Angela is ready to work with programmers around the world to make her dreams and the dreams of others a technological reality. Angela has been looking for ways to integreate her love for technology with her love for dancing for years. Right now, Angela is learning the tools to launch a software application for choreographers to stage whole pieces before the rehearsal process even starts! In order to execute such software, Angela is learning the folloing coding methods:
  1. JavaScript
  2. CSS
  3. html

Angela is a classically trained choreographer, specializing in the art forms of ballet, modern, and imporvisation. Angela composes pieces to stand-out musical scores where dancers push their bodies to the limits. In each piece Angela produces, each dancer gets creative voice and choice on their involvement of the choreographic process and the performance of the work.

Vision

STUDIO6 would like to accomplish the following:

  • Community Outreach to provide access to art education to low income learners.
  • Integration of technology in dance.
  • Normalizing touring as a dance company both nationally and internationally
  • The building of a community of artists ready inspire generations to come
